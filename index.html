What is upcycling?

Upcycling means turning something old or damaged into something new and more valuable — not melting it down, but reimagining it. A worn-out leather bag becomes a wallet, a keychain, or a cardholder. The material lives on, just in a better form.

✂️

Deconstruct

Salvage the best panels from a worn piece

✨

Redesign

Cut and shape into a new product

🏷️

Revalue

Sold again at a meaningful price

Why it matters

🌿

Producing new leather uses enormous water and chemicals. Upcycling skips all of that.

♻️

Leather takes 50+ years to decompose in a landfill. One upcycled wallet removes that waste permanently.

🤲

You get a handcrafted item at a fraction of the cost of new — made by local Bahir Dar artisans.

✅

Every piece processed keeps leather out of landfill and supports local Bahir Dar artisans.
